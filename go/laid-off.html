Real Talk

Straight answers for a hard season.

“I just lost my income — I can’t spend money on school.”

Start with the free WIOA check before you spend a dollar: Workforce Solutions funding exists precisely for laid-off workers and can cover training for people who qualify. Local businesses also sponsor students through our Sponsor-a-Student program.

“I need to be job-hunting, not in a classroom.”

Do both — the program is about 12 weeks, and we help with your résumé and interviews with East Texas dental offices before you finish. You’d be job-hunting with a credential instead of an apology.

“Starting over at my age feels impossible.”

It isn’t, and you wouldn’t be alone — our classes are full of adults restarting. Small classes, instructors in the room, no dental background required. Reliability and life experience are hiring assets in a dental office.
