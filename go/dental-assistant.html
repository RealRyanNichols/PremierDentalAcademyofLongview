Real Talk

The three worries we hear most — answered straight.

"I don't have time for school."

This isn't a two-year program — it's about 12 weeks, built for people who work, parent, or both. Ask us for the current schedule; most students are surprised it fits.

"I can't afford it."

Pay $3,000 in full, or go on a plan: $500 down holds your seat, then the balance in simple weekly or monthly payments — no big lump sum. Klarna, Afterpay, and Affirm work too.

"I've never worked in dental."

Almost nobody in our classroom has. The program starts from zero, and your instructors correct your technique in person until it clicks.
