Questions, answered.

Do I have to pay anything to apply?+

No. Applying is completely free. The only cost is the $100–$150 state instructor fee, and you only pay that after we've reviewed, interviewed, and approved you.

How much does it pay?+

New instructors start at $20/hour, with room to grow as you take on more cohorts and responsibility.

What's the time commitment?+

Each class runs 12 weeks. We ask instructors to commit to at least one full cohort (12 weeks) by contract so students have a consistent teacher start to finish. Beyond that, it's flexible — teach more cohorts or take a break.

What experience do I need?+

At least 5 years as a Registered Dental Assistant and a current, up-to-date Texas RDA license in good standing.

What is the state fee for?+

Texas requires instructors and their school affiliation to be approved by the state. The fee (roughly $100–$150) is paid directly to the state to get your instructor license and company info approved. We'll walk you through it once you're approved on our end.
