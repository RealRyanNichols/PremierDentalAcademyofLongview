Common questions, honest answers.

How much does it cost to sponsor a student?+
A full seat is $3,000 (the pay-in-full tuition). You can also sponsor part of a seat — $500 covers a student's down payment and gets her started, and any amount is applied straight to tuition.
Is a sponsorship tax-deductible?+
We can't give tax advice. PDA is a private career school, not a 501(c)(3), so this isn't a charitable donation — but many local businesses treat sponsorships as an ordinary business expense (advertising / workforce development), especially with the public recognition we provide. We invoice your business and give you full documentation; ask your CPA how it fits your situation.
Do I pick the student?+
Your call. Browse the board above and pick someone, or tell Amanda what matters to you (your town, a dental office growing its own assistant, a single parent getting a restart) and she'll match you. She personally talks with every student before they're listed.
Where does the money go?+
100% to that student's tuition at Premier Dental Academy — we invoice you directly and apply it to her account. If your student can't finish, we'll talk to you before anything else happens with the funds.
I'm a student — is this a loan? What's the catch?+
Not a loan, and no catch: a sponsor pays some or all of your tuition because they want another trained dental assistant in East Texas. Your part is showing up and doing the work. Applying is free, and nothing about you is made public without your written OK.
