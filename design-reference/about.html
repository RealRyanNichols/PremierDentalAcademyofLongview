Our story

Real software. Real instructors. Real East Texas jobs.

Most dental-assistant programs hand you a textbook and hope. We do it differently. From your first week, you practice on PDA Practice Pro and ChairSide — interactive trainers that mirror the systems offices use every day. You chart real cases, submit real-format claims, and run a real schedule before you ever clock in.

The result: graduates who walk into their first office already knowing what to do — and hiring dentists who ask for PDA-trained candidates by name.

  • Hundreds of hours on real-grade practice-management software.
  • Small classes and direct instruction in Longview.
  • Job-placement help across East Texas.
