FAQ

Honest answers.

How long until I'm working?

Our RDA program runs about 12 weeks — in person at our Longview campus or online from home. Most students start interviewing in week 10 and have offers within 2 weeks of completion.

Do I need previous dental experience?

No. About 70% of our students come in with no prior dental background. The curriculum and trainers are designed for someone walking in cold.

What makes the PDA trainers different from "online courses"?

Online courses show you what software looks like. Our trainers are software — fully interactive, with 25 sample patients, charts you mark, claims you submit, prescriptions you write.

Will my certificate be recognized by Texas offices?

Yes. Our curriculum aligns with the Texas State Board of Dental Examiners RDA registration requirements, and many hiring-partner offices specifically request PDA-trained candidates.

Can I make payments instead of paying upfront?

Yes for In-Person — $3,000 paid in full, or $3,500 on a plan ($500 down, weekly or monthly). Online is on sale at $397 one-time (reg $997). We also accept Texas Workforce Solutions vouchers, GI Bill / veterans benefits, and WIOA funding.

Where is the school located?

Our campus is at 2800 Gilmer Rd, Suite 106, Longview, TX 75604. Students attend from across East Texas, and trainer access is online so you can practice anywhere.

Ready for the career you actually want?

In 12 weeks you can be a working RDA in East Texas. The first step is filling out the application — no payment required to apply.
