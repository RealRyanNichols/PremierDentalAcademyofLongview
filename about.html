From the founder

Why I run Premier Dental Academy.

I'll be straight with you: I'm not a doctor, and I didn't come from some corporate training company. I sat exactly where you are right now — I took this same course, at this same school.

I earned my RDA and went to work chairside in an East Texas dental office. I learned fast that the hardest part of the first day isn't the dentistry — it's the software, the charting, the insurance, and the rhythm of a real practice that no classroom ever taught me.

I put my head down, did the work, and built a real career from it. Eventually I was asked if I wanted to take this school over — so I bought it. That's how I became the owner: not handed anything, just a regular person who went through this program and made it work.

Now I run Premier Dental Academy with one mission: every graduate walks into their first office already fluent — because I lived the gap, and I'm not about to send my students into it.

That's why we built our own practice-management trainers from scratch — Practice Pro and ChairSide — why our curriculum is built around real workflows, and why we partner with East Texas dental offices directly so students go from graduation to working in days, not months.

If that's the kind of school you want to learn at, I'd love to meet you. — Amanda
