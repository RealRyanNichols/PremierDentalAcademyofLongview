# Texas RDA standards — ground truth for course content (researched July 11, 2026)

Source of truth for what the PDA courses must prepare students for. Used by the
course-content depth passes. Verify anything time-sensitive at tsbde.texas.gov
before stating it to students.

## What Texas actually requires for RDA registration (TSBDE)

- **Path 1 (what PDA's course serves):** successfully complete a **course of
  instruction and examination administered by a TSBDE-approved provider**. The
  approved RDA course/exam covers three domains: **Radiology, Infection Control,
  and Texas Jurisprudence** (commonly delivered as a 6-hour minimum course; PDA's
  12-week program goes far deeper than the minimum).
- **Path 2:** hold a current **DANB certificate** and pass the **TSBDE
  Jurisprudence Assessment** within one year of application.
- **Also required with the application:** Basic Life Support (BLS) with hands-on
  manikin demonstration + written assessment, and (since Oct 1, 2021) a
  **human trafficking prevention course** approved by Texas HHS.
- **X-ray certification** is the core of RDA registration — a dental assistant in
  Texas must be RDA-registered to expose radiographs.
- **CE after registration** (Rule §114.12): 6 hours/year covering assistant
  duties, at least 3 clinical.

## What this means for course emphasis

The exam-critical spine of the 12-week course is:
1. **Radiology** (weeks 5–6: chapters 38–41 + reviews) — physics/safety/ALARA,
   technique, errors, legal/QA, intraoral imaging.
2. **Infection control** (week 3: chapters 19–21, 23) — chain of infection,
   standard precautions, disinfection levels, sterilization + monitoring,
   hazard/waste handling.
3. **Texas jurisprudence** (week 1: chapter 5) — Dental Practice Act concepts,
   supervision levels, delegation, records, consent, mandatory reporting.
   NEVER state specific Texas rule numbers/fees as fact in lessons — point to
   tsbde.texas.gov; concepts and definitions are fine.

Everything else (anatomy, morphology, materials, chairside procedure, front
office) builds employability and follows the standard **Modern Dental Assisting
(Bird & Robinson)** chapter curriculum, which the Kajabi course mirrored.

## Content rules for every lesson (restated)

- Depth target for full chapters: comparable to a textbook-chapter walkthrough
  (the imported week 7–10 lessons are the exemplars).
- No fabricated Texas statutes, rule numbers, fees, or scope-of-practice claims.
- BLS + human-trafficking course + application steps: mention that they exist as
  state requirements, direct to tsbde.texas.gov for current specifics.
- Where hands-on practice matters, link the real tools: /tools/practice-exam,
  /tools/practice-pro, /skills-lab, /tools/flashcards.
