Questions people ask

Is this for total beginners?

Yes. The Welcome module starts with a self-assessment and a goal date. If you've never set foot in a dental office, start at Track 1 and follow the order.

Is this the same as the 12-week RDA program?

No — the Vault is the self-paced online companion. The full program includes hands-on classroom training at our Longview campus. Many students use the Vault to prepare for or supplement the program.

How long do I have access?

You keep access. Your progress is saved in your member area and you can revisit any lesson.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes — a PDA Career Vault completion certificate when you finish all four tracks. (The RDA credential itself comes from the state of Texas; Track 1 gets you ready for that exam.)

I already bought it — where do I go?

Sign in and open /learn — the Vault is on your course list.
