Practice questions

Smart questions to ask THEM

  • What does a typical day look like for your assistants?
  • What software does the office run? (You can say you trained on practice-management software.)
  • What would make someone successful in this role in the first 90 days?
  • Are there chances to grow — expanded functions, ortho, front desk?

Before you go

  • Dress clean and professional (scrubs are fine if asked; otherwise business casual).
  • Bring your resume, your PDA certificate, and any certifications.
  • Arrive 10 minutes early. Smile, firm handshake, eye contact.
  • Have one short story ready about helping a person or working on a team.
  • Send a quick thank-you text or email the same day.
