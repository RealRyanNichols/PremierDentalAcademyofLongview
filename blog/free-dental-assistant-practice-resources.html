One page, everything free

All of these live in the PDA toolbox, but this is the plain-English map. Everything below is genuinely free to use right now. You do not have to be enrolled, and you do not have to give us your email to try them. If a tool helps, great — use it. If it makes you want the real thing, we're here for that too.

Stage 1 — Deciding if this is for you

Before you spend a dollar or a weekend, these help you decide with clear eyes:

  • Is It For Me? — a quick, honest quiz about whether dental assisting fits your life.
  • Career Paths Explorer — where dental assisting can take you, and what each step tends to pay.
  • Funding Finder — six questions to see if WIOA or other funding could cover your tuition.
  • Take-Home Pay Calculator — what an East Texas dental assistant paycheck really looks like.
  • Savings Starter — a simple plan to save your $500 down payment.
  • Schedule Planner — see how class fits around your job, your kids, and your life.
  • The Family Talk — how to explain this decision to the people who worry about you.

Stage 2 — Learning & practicing

This is the heart of it. These are the tools we're proudest of, because you can practice the real work before anyone hands you a real patient:

  • Free Practice Exam — sample RDA-style questions with instant scoring.
  • RDA Flashcards — instruments, terms, and infection control — flip and learn.
  • PDA Practice Pro — a Dentrix-style practice-management trainer with a real tooth chart.
  • PDA ChairSide — a clinical workflow trainer for procedures, trays, and assisting steps.
  • How To Chart — dental charting explained the way offices actually do it.
  • Study Plan Builder — a week-by-week RDA study plan around your schedule.
Start with Practice Pro.

If you only click one thing, make it Practice Pro — charting on a real tooth chart is the moment this career stops being an idea and starts feeling real. Curious how it works first? Read inside PDA Practice Pro.

Stage 3 — Getting hired

Training gets you ready; these help you land the job:

  • Resume Builder — a dental-assistant resume that reads right to office managers.
  • Interview Prep — the questions East Texas offices ask, and strong answers.
  • First 30 Days — how to crush your first month in a dental office.
  • Resource Hub — guides, checklists, and links for every stage of the journey.

And the Skills Lab

The Skills Lab is a whole practice wing: competencies, simulators, and quizzes. Build a tray in the tray builder, drill terminology and topics in the quizzes, and walk an operatory in the virtual dental office.
