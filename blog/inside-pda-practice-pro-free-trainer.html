Inside PDA Practice Pro: train on a real dental office — free

Most schools show you slides of dental software. We built the software — and you can open it right now, no signup, no card. Here is what is inside and why it gets our graduates hired faster.

Why software is the skill offices actually test

A dental office runs on two screens: the front desk (scheduling, payments, insurance) and the chair (charting, notes, prescriptions). New assistants who can already work those screens save an office weeks of onboarding — which is exactly why local offices told us it is the first thing they look for. Practice Pro exists so you walk in already knowing where everything lives.

What you can do inside Practice Pro

🦷 Chart on an anatomical tooth chart.

Every tooth is drawn with real cusps, roots, and gingival anatomy. Mark caries, composites, crowns, root canals, implants, and missing teeth — the same way you will in the software real offices run.

📊 Run a 6-point perio chart.

Pocket depths, bleeding on probing, mobility, and furcation per tooth — with automatic AAP 2017 stage and grade calculation and a plain-English clinical interpretation.

💊 Write prescriptions with an allergy guard.

A 22-drug dental formulary — and if you try to prescribe amoxicillin to a penicillin-allergic patient, the app stops you with a red warning, the same safety logic real e-prescribing requires.

📅 Run the schedule like the front desk does.

Four operatories, 30-minute slots, color-coded by provider. Drag any appointment to reschedule and text-confirm with a tap.

📝 Write real clinical notes.

SmartDoc gives you 15 ready-to-use SOAP templates — Prophy, Restorative, RCT, Crown Seat, SRP and more. Saved notes lock for legal compliance, with amendment tracking.

💰 Master the insurance side.

Generate ADA-format claims, post EOBs, and auto-write-off contractual adjustments — the part of the job new assistants struggle with most, handled before your first day.
