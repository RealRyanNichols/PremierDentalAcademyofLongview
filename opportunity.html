The path is shorter than people think

Most healthcare careers take years. Dental assisting doesn't — our program is 12 weeks of training, then you test for your Texas registration.

Texas registration requires a state-approved course and exam covering radiology, infection control, and jurisprudence — that's exactly what we prepare you for. Current requirements: tsbde.texas.gov.

The demand is local and real

East Texas dental offices — Longview, Tyler, Marshall, Kilgore, and the towns between — hire assistants year-round, and offices ask us for graduates by name. Texas employs roughly 34,000 dental assistants, and the field keeps growing as offices add chairs.
