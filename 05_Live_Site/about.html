From the founder

Why I started Premier Dental Academy.

I spent over twenty years in dental offices across East Texas. In every single one of them, I watched new dental assistants struggle on day one — not because they didn't know the dentistry, but because they didn't know the software, the workflow, the rhythm of a real practice.

The schools sent them with textbook knowledge and zero practice. The offices then spent weeks training them on EagleSoft, Dentrix, charting systems, and insurance — time the office couldn't afford and the new hire felt terrible about.

I knew there had to be a better way. So in 2024 I started Premier Dental Academy of Longview with one mission: build a school where every graduate walks into their first office already fluent.

That's why we built our own practice management trainers from scratch — Practice Pro and ChairSide. That's why our curriculum focuses on real workflows. That's why we partner with East Texas dental offices directly so our students go from graduation to working in days, not months.

If that mission matters to you, I'd love to meet you. — Amanda
