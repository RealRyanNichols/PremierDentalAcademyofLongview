What sets us apart

The only RDA program with real practice management training.

Other schools teach you what a tooth chart is. We let you mark caries on 25 patients before lunch.

🦷

Anatomical tooth chart

Each tooth drawn with real cusps, roots, and gingival anatomy. Mark caries, composites, crowns, RCTs, implants, missing teeth — exactly like the software in real offices.

📊

6-point AAP perio chart

Full pocket depths, bleeding-on-probing, mobility, furcation per tooth. Auto-calculates AAP 2017 stage + grade with clinical interpretation. Nothing on the consumer market does this.

💊

Prescription pad with allergy guard

22-drug dental formulary. Try to prescribe amoxicillin to a penicillin-allergic patient and the app stops you with a red warning — the same safety logic real e-prescribing requires.

📅

Drag-drop operatory schedule

4 operatories, 30-min slots, color-coded by provider. Drag any appointment to reschedule. Text-confirm with a single tap. Built exactly the way the front desk uses it.

📝

SmartDoc clinical notes

15 ready-to-use note templates (Prophy, Restorative, RCT, Crown Seat, SRP…) with the SOAP framework pre-filled. Saved notes lock for legal compliance with amendment tracking.

💰

Real insurance workflow

Generate ADA-format claims, post EOBs, auto-write-off contractual adjustments. Master the insurance side of the practice — the part new RDAs struggle with most.

Curriculum

12 weeks. 12 modules. Real skills.

Every module pairs classroom instruction with hands-on practice in the trainer.

01

Patient check-in & greeting

HIPAA, registration, insurance verification, schedule lookup, financial agreement.

02

Medical history & vitals

Allergies, meds, conditions, premedication, BP / pulse / SpO2 with clinical alerts.

03

Tooth charting

Surface marking, restoration types, missing teeth, primary vs permanent dentition.

04

Periodontal charting

6-point pocket depths, bleeding-on-probing, mobility, AAP staging & grading.

05

Radiology & imaging

FMX layout, bitewings, panorex, intraoral photo series, safety + technique.

06

Treatment planning

ADA codes, phased plans, insurance estimates, signed consent, case acceptance.

07

Insurance & claims

Verification, ADA claim form, EOB matching, contractual write-offs.

08

Ledger & financials

Posting procedures, payments, adjustments, walkout statements, A/R aging.

09

Patient communication

Texts, calls, emails, voicemails, recall reminders, post-op check-ins.

10

Scheduling & recall

Operatory grid, drag-drop appointments, no-show handling, recall automation.

11

Clinical documentation

SmartDoc templates, locked notes, amendments, lab cases, prescriptions.

12

Practice analytics

Production, recall retention, no-show rate, case acceptance, hygiene %.

FAQ

Honest answers.

How long until I'm working? +
Our RDA program runs about 12 weeks — In-Person at our Longview campus or Online from home. Most students start interviewing in week 10 and have offers within 2 weeks of completion.
Do I need previous dental experience? +
No. About 70% of our students come in with no prior dental background. The curriculum and the trainers are designed for someone walking in cold. If you can show up, want to learn, and care about patients, you'll do well.
What makes the PDA trainers different from "online courses"? +
Online courses show you what software looks like. Our trainers are software — fully interactive, with 25 sample patients, charts you actually mark, claims you actually submit, prescriptions you actually write. It's the difference between watching cooking videos and standing at the stove.
Will my certificate be recognized by Texas dental offices? +
Yes. Our curriculum aligns with the Texas State Board of Dental Examiners RDA registration requirements. Graduates are positioned to register as RDAs and immediately start applying for positions. Many of our hiring partner offices specifically request PDA-trained candidates.
Can I make payments instead of paying upfront? +
Yes for In-Person ($1,997) — payment plans available. Online is on sale right now: $397 one-time (regularly $997) — non-refundable at this price, but if it isn't right for you we'll transfer the full $397 as credit toward the In-Person program. We also accept Texas Workforce Solutions vouchers, GI Bill / veterans benefits, and WIOA workforce funding.
Where is the school located? +
Our campus is in Longview, TX. Students attend from Longview, Tyler, Marshall, Kilgore, Henderson, Gladewater, and surrounding East Texas towns. Trainer access is online, so you can practice from anywhere with internet.
What if I don't like it after enrolling? +
Online ($397 sale): non-refundable — but transferable in full to In-Person credit if it isn't right for you. Your money is never lost.

In-Person ($1,997): pro-rated refund per Texas state proprietary-school guidelines. Submit withdrawal in writing (email is fine); refunds process within 30 days.
