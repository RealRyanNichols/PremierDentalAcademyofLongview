Texas state requirements

The three things Texas requires — built in.

To register as a Registered Dental Assistant with the Texas State Board of Dental Examiners (TSBDE), you complete three required components. They're part of the program.

🩻
Radiology

Take and mount diagnostic dental X-rays safely.

⚖️
Jurisprudence

Know the Texas dental laws and rules that govern your work.

🧼
Infection control

Sterilization and safety that protects every patient.

Students are responsible for completing applicable Texas state requirements and registering with the TSBDE. PDA does not guarantee licensure or employment.
